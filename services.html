Services & Dental Technologies

100SMILES Dental Care offers treatments for children, adults, and seniors, supported by modern technology.

Our Dental Services

Routine Wellness Check-Ups

Preventive check-ups, cleanings, and early detection for all ages.

Children's Dentistry

Gentle care for young patients, including education and protective treatments.

Dental Implants

Strong, natural-looking implants to replace missing teeth.

Orthodontics

Braces and alignment options for teens and adults.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Whitening, veneers, and treatments to brighten your smile.

Root Canal Treatment

Comfort-focused treatments to save damaged teeth.

Emergency Care

Same-day emergency appointments when possible.

Nutritional Guidance

Support and advice to build healthy lifelong habits.

Our New Dental Technologies

Digital X-Rays

Digital X-Rays provide high detail with much lower radiation compared to traditional imaging.

Intraoral Cameras

Tiny cameras allow patients to see exactly what the dentist sees during examinations.

Laser Dentistry

Used for gum treatment, whitening, and cavity removal with greater comfort and precision.

CAD/CAM Technology

Create same-day crowns and restorations with perfect precision and faster turnaround.

3D Printing

Used to create accurate dental models and surgical guides for advanced procedures.

Teledentistry

Virtual consultations for busy families or patients who prefer remote advice.
