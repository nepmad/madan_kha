Meet Our Team

Our dentists, hygienists, and support staff work together to provide gentle, age-appropriate dental care for children, adults, and seniors.

Dr. Johanna Grey

DDS, Orthodontics

Dr. Johanna Grey focuses on orthodontic care, helping teens and adults create healthy, straight smiles in a calm and supportive environment.

Dr. Emily Carter

Pediatric Dentist

Dr. Emily Carter specialises in children’s dentistry. She uses kid-friendly language and a gentle approach so children feel safe during their visits.

Dr. Benedict Sloan

DMD

With many years of experience, Dr. Benedict Sloan supports adults and seniors with restorative, cosmetic, and emergency dental treatments.

Richard Attam

BDS – Dental Hygienist

Richard provides thorough yet gentle cleanings and hygiene advice, helping families maintain healthy teeth and gums between visits.

April Kepner

Receptionist

April is the friendly first contact for patients of all ages. She manages appointments and works to make every visit run smoothly.
