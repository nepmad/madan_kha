About 100SMILES Dental Care

Located at 33 Fantail Street, Razole, 100SMILES Dental Care is a modern clinic welcoming patients of all ages from young children visiting for their first check-up to older adults needing ongoing care.

With over 50 years of combined experience, our team provides routine check-ups, children's dentistry, orthodontics, dental implants, cosmetic treatments, and emergency care. We focus on using friendly language, clear explanations, and a calming environment so that everyone feels comfortable.

We also offer nutritional guidance and oral hygiene education, helping families build healthy habits at home.

Why families choose us

  • Dedicated paediatric dentist for children
  • Gentle care for nervous or older patients
  • Modern technologies for accurate, comfortable treatment
  • Online booking and weekend hours
  • Community events like free check-up days
